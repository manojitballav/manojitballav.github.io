About

Product leader specializing in consumer electronics

Manojit Ballav, also known as Mono, is a product leader with 9+ years of experience in consumer electronics, smart TV platforms, and connected devices. He thrives at the intersection of hardware and software, building products that millions of people use every day.

Currently at Lumio, he focuses on home projectors, the TLDR content discovery platform, and product operations. Lumio has built QD-Mini LED TVs, QLED smart TVs, and home projectors — achieving $1M GMV within 2 months of launch.

Previously at Xiaomi (2017–2024), he spent 7 years helping build India's #1 smart TV brand for 22 consecutive quarters (Q2 2018 – Q4 2023). He helped build PatchWall — one of India's earliest smart TV content aggregation platforms, serving 6M+ monthly active users — and managed the product lifecycle for smart TVs, streaming devices, soundbars, and the Xiaomi Pad 6.

His focus areas include smart TV product strategy, CTV advertising, FAST channels, display technologies (QD-Mini LED, QLED, OLED), AI-driven content discovery, and the connected device ecosystem in India.

Manojit Ballav is also the author of an IEEE research publication, reflecting his technical background in computer science and engineering.
